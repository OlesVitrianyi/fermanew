Sweet Mavka Script – Latin & Cyrillic is long, clean font with latin and Cyrillic support.
It’s a very versatile font that works great in large and small sizes.

Sweet Mavka Script is perfect for branding projects, home-ware designs,
product packaging, magazine headers – or simply as a stylish text overlay to any background image.

Sweet Mavka Script free for personal and commercial use.
